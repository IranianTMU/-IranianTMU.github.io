Voices of Tehranto

The Iranian Students Association at Toronto Metropolitan University. A space for community, cultural resilience, and solidarity.

A Note on Our Space

ISAMET is committed to the safety and privacy of our members. To protect our international students and their families back home, all of our events operate under a strict explicit-consent photo policy. We maintain zero tolerance for surveillance or harassment.

We gather to honor the resilience of the Iranian people. You are welcome here exactly as you are.

2026/2027 Event Calendar

September

Tirgan "Water & Sharbat" Quad Pop-Up

Location: TMU Quad | 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Join us between classes to celebrate the mid-summer festival of water. We'll be weaving traditional Tir-o-Bad bracelets and serving ice-cold Khakshir and Sekanjabin.

October

Faculty Speed Networking

Location: SLC Sandbox | 6:00 PM

Connect with Iranian-Canadian faculty across STEM, Arts, and Humanities in a casual, 12-minute rotating table format. Mentorship, career advice, and community processing.

November

Shahnameh Immersive Night

Location: Campus Theater Space | 7:00 PM

A multimedia dive into Ferdowsi's epic. Featuring high-resolution projection mapping of Persian miniatures, ambient soundscapes, and poetry that preserved a language.

December

Tri-Campus Shabe Yalda Gala

Co-hosted with UTIRAN & York | Location TBA

Celebrate the winter solstice with a massive joint gala. Featuring a traditional Sofreh, Fal-e Hafez readings, and a local Tehranto DJ.

January

"Bloody January" Memorial Vigil

Location: TMU Student Centre | Evening

A solemn, highly secure candlelight vigil honoring the victims of the January massacres and Flight PS752. Crisis counselors and decompression spaces will be available on-site.

March

Nowruz & Chaharshanbe Suri Pop-Up

Location: TMU Quad | 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Welcoming the spring and the Persian New Year. Stop by our Haft-Sin display for traditional sweets, acoustic music, and community renewal.
